section 4b

1How could one locate the venerated and secret hexagon which housed Him?

2Someone proposed a regressive method: To locate book A, consult first book B which indicates A's position; to locate book B, consult first a book C, and so on to infinity...

3In adventures such as these, I have squandered and wasted my years.

4It does not seem unlikely to me that there is a total book on some shelf of the universe 3; I pray to the unknown gods that a man—just one, even though it were thousands of years ago!—may have examined and read it.

5If honor and wisdom and happiness are not for me, let them be for others.
