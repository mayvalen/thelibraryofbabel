upstairs
section 4c

1Let heaven exist, though my place be in hell.

2Let me be outraged and annihilated, but for one instant, in one being, let Your enormous Library be justified.

3The impious maintain that nonsense is normal in the Library and that the reasonable (and even humble and pure coherence) is an almost miraculous exception.

4They speak (I know) of the "feverish Library whose chance volumes are constantly in danger of changing into others and affirm, negate and confuse everything like a delirious divinity."

5These words, which not only denounce the disorder but exemplify it as well, notoriously prove their authors' abominable taste and desperate ignorance.
