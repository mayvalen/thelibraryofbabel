upstairs
section 6a

1I believe I have mentioned suicides, more and more frequent with the years.

2Perhaps my old age and fearfulness deceive me, but I suspect that the human species - the unique species - is about to be extinguished,

3but the Library will endure:

4illuminated, solitary, infinite, perfectly motionless, equipped with precious volumes, useless, incorruptible, secret.

5I have just written the word "infinite".
