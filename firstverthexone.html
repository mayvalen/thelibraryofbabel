This thinker observed that all the books, no matter how diverse they might be,
are made up of the same elements: the space, the period, the comma, the twenty-two
letters of the alphabet.

Tihs thknier obvresed taht all the bskoo, no mettar how direvse tehy mhgit be,
are mdae up of the smae eltnemes: the secap, the peoird, the cammo, the twe-ytntwo
leettrs of the alebahpt.
