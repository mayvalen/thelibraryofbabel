upstairs
section 6b

1I have not interpolated this adjective out of rhetorical habit;

2I say that it is not illogical to think that the world is infinite.

3Those who judge it to be limited postulate that in remote places the corridors and stairways and hexagons can conceivably come to an end

4which is absurd

5Those who imagine it to be without limit forget that the possible number of books does have such a limit.
