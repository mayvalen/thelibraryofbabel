downstairs
section 5c

1You who read me, are You sure of understanding my language?)

2The methodical task of writing distracts me from the present state of men.

3The certitude that everything has been written negates us or turns us into phantoms.

4I know of districts in which the young men prostrate themselves before books and kiss their pages in a barbarous manner, but they do not know how to decipher a single letter.

5Epidemics, heretical conflicts, peregrinations which inevitably degenerate into banditry, have decimated the population.
